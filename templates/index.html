About this application

This is a comprehensive application allowing prospective college students, workers, and others find critical information about their futures by allowing them to sort fields based on factors such as salaries, level of education, and more! We are better than the competitors because there aren't any!

Here is the main source used for this project:

The NSCG is a biennial survey that provides data on the characteristics of the nation's college graduates, with a focus on those in the science and engineering workforce.

Guide: How to use this application

Simply enter in a major into the box. You will soon be able to add other factors, such as salary. Then, click the search box and you will see results. Enjoy!
